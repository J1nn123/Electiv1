That time they ran out of Kwek-kwek...

Posted by: Xel
Posted on: 09/26/24

Ever experieced what is like to not be able to eat your favourite food after a long day at school? Well, that happened to me multiple times over. Whenever I had the money, I'd often spend much of it on my favourite snack, Kwek-kwek. Normally, I'd by 4 at a time, which is pretty expensive. Before, Kwek-kwek was 5 pesos cheape, and for the same price, I could buy 6 pices. I love how simple it is. It's literally just boiled egg wrapped in flour and friend. The ketchup or sauce it wass makes it really good though, and a it can make your day or otherwise. So, I was pretty bummed when the canteen ran out (and I was too lazy to walk myself out of the campus to buy some from the vendor). In the end, I didn't buy anything, and I got hungrier by the day as a result. Thank God we had good food at home though, cuz my the time I got back, my stomach was rumbling!
